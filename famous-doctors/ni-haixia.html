Ni Hai-Xia

Classical formulas for modern lives

Contemporary · 1954–2012 CE

Biography

Dr. Ni Hai-Xia trained in Taiwan and later founded Han Tang Chinese Medicine in the United States. Through seminars, books, and video lessons he introduced English-speaking students to the intricacies of the Shang Han Lun and Jinkui Yaolue.

He combined astronomy, channel theory, and classical herbalism with vivid storytelling, making the tradition approachable without diluting its rigor.

Clinical Focus

Classical prescription application, seasonal health management, and patient education across global communities.

Key Contributions

  • Created multimedia courses that made classical Chinese texts accessible to English-speaking practitioners.
  • Emphasised personal cultivation, astronomy, and calendar science in diagnosis.
  • Inspired an international network of clinicians dedicated to formula-based care.

Recommended Reading

  • Classical Formulas for Today’s Practitioner
  • Between Heaven and Earth: Understanding the Universe of Chinese Medicine

Legacy Note

Dr. Ni’s teachings continue through study groups, digital archives, and dedicated clinics. His warmth and precision remind us that the classics live through the people who teach and apply them.
