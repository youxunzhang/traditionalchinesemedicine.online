Dr. Ni Hai-Xia

Founder of Han Tang Chinese Medicine. An energetic bridge between the classical texts and modern seekers, Dr. Ni showed students how astronomy, meridians, and herbal formulas weave together to support real people.

1954–2012 Classical Formulas Global Educator

Life & Milestones

From Taiwan to the world

Dr. Ni practised in Taiwan before moving to Florida, where he founded Han Tang Chinese Medicine and began teaching internationally.

Early Training

Studied classical texts under mentors steeped in the Han dynasty lineage, with a special devotion to the Shang Han Lun and Jinkui Yaolue.

Han Tang Clinic

Established the Han Tang clinic in Orlando, Florida, treating patients with customised classical prescriptions and acupuncture.

Global Teaching

Launched seminars across North America, Europe, and Asia, pairing vivid case studies with astronomical observations.

Signature Approach

How Dr. Ni taught

A balance of rigorous classics and everyday language made his lectures unforgettable.

Story-driven lectures

Complex theories were broken into vivid stories—patients became “friends” whose patterns we followed from symptom to symptom.

Astronomy & Calendrics

He tracked heavenly stems, earthly branches, and seasonal qi to explain why the same formula behaves differently throughout the year.

Channel craftsmanship

Needling and herbal strategies were taught together, always referencing the channels and how herbs travel along them.
